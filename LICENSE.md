## Free for the Home

This technology exists to help people. Not corporations. People.

### Always Free

- **Personal use** — Run it on your computer. Learn. Explore. Create. Forever free.
- **Home business** — Building something from your garage, kitchen table, or spare room? Free. This is for you. Build your dreams.
- **Open source projects** — If you're sharing with the community, we're sharing with you. Free.
- **Education & research** — Students, teachers, researchers. Free.
- **Non-profits & charities** — Making the world better? Free.

### What "Home Business" Means

If you're a person (not a corporation) running a business:
- From your home
- As a sole proprietor
- As a small partnership
- As a small LLC you own

...you're free. No fees. No licensing. No permission needed.

**The line**: If you have a dedicated office building, a board of directors, or venture capital funding, you're probably not a home business. Contact us.

## Why This Approach

The AI industry charges $20-200/month for access to technology that costs them pennies to serve. They gatekeep capabilities behind paywalls. They harvest your data. They decide what you're allowed to think.

We believe:
- AGI should be accessible to everyone
- Home businesses should compete with corporations
- People should own their tools, not rent them
- The future should be built from the bottom up

This license reflects those values.
